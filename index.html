Co-Founder & Full-Stack Developer | Manny.lk

               

                  I led the full development cycle of Manny.lk, from initial concept and architecture to deployment and scaling. The platform features a Next.js front end with TypeScript, deployed via Vercel for seamless CI/CD. It integrates a serverless PostgreSQL database on Neon and leverages Vercel Blob Storage for product assets. Key capabilities include a custom admin dashboard, dynamic product routing, responsive Tailwind CSS design, and an optimized checkout process supporting Cash on Delivery and bank transfers. Backed by Cloudflare CDN and DNS, the platform ensures fast, reliable performance across Sri Lanka. The project reflects my focus on scalability, maintainability, and cohesive branding, bridging real-world business workflows with modern, production-ready technology.

Solar Power Adoption Analysis

               

                  As part of my coursework for the Statistical Distribution and Inferences module, I was responsible for data preprocessing and analysis using R. I developed a function to parse and convert raw, text-based survey responses into a continuous numeric dataset. I then visualized the empirical distribution of monthly electricity usage and compared it against a theoretical normal distribution, allowing me to identify skewness and formally model the consumption patterns.
